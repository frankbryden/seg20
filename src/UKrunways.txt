
UNITED KINGDOM A Member of the European Union CIVIL AVIATION AUTHORITY AERODROME CERTIFICATE ABERDEEN
Certificate Reference: UK: EGPD - 001
Pursuant to Regulation (EC) No 216/2008 of the European Parliament and of the Council and the Commission Regulation (EU) No 139/2014 for the time being in force and subject to the conditions specified below, The United Kingdom Civil Aviation Authority hereby certifies that:
Aberdeen International Airport Ltd Aberdeen Airport Dyce Aberdeen AB21 7DU
is authorised to operate Aberdeen Aerodrome in accordance with the provisions of Regulation (EC) No 216/2008 and its Implementing Rules, the aerodrome certification basis, the terms of the certificate and the aerodrome manual.
This certificate shall remain valid for an unlimited duration, unless it is surrendered or revoked.
Date of original issue: 22 January 2015
Signed: ......  For the UK Civil Aviation Authority
